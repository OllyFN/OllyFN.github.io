2023 Roadmap

Web development

I embarked on my web development journey using React, a powerful library known for its simplicity and component-based architecture. With React, I crafted interactive and dynamic user interfaces, leveraging its flexibility to handle complex data flows effortlessly. I prefer using Mantine as my go-to component library, but in smaller projects, I rely on PostCSS for lightweight styling solutions.

NoSQL databases

I delved into NoSQL databases, specifically Firebase, honing my skills in handling non-relational data structures. I integrated Firebase with Node.js and Express, creating RESTful APIs that efficiently managed data. This experience laid the foundation for my expertise in server-side data handling and database design.

Software development

My journey in software development began with Electron, enabling me to seamlessly merge my front-end web skills. After experimenting with Electron, I transitioned to Tauri, which led me to learn Rust. Despite my initial challenges, I successfully created a lightweight task tracker with enhanced functionalities. This journey enhanced my problem-solving skills and introduced me to the world of low-level programming.

SQL databases

My exploration of back-end development extended to SQL and MySQL databases. I utilized my knowledge to create full-stack applications, mastering the art of handling relational data and designing efficient database structures. This experience deepened my understanding of database management and optimized data retrieval processes.

Testing frameworks

Currently, I haven't dived into testing frameworks, but it's a significant milestone on my roadmap. I'm eager to explore and master testing frameworks to ensure the reliability and robustness of my applications. This exciting phase of my journey is just around the corner, and I look forward to incorporating rigorous testing practices into my development process to create bug-free and dependable software solutions. Stay tuned for my progress in this area!

The future

As 2024 approaches, I'm committed to mastering Rust and exploring cloud technologies. My goal is to excel in Rust through hands-on practice, deepening my understanding of its syntax and concepts. In parallel, I'm excited to dive into cloud solutions, specifically Amazon Web Services and Azure. Additionally, I'll venture into serverless computing with AWS Lambda and Azure Functions, streamlining cloud-based app development and elevating my skill set as a developer.
